📈 What is SIP Suitability?

Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) suitability helps you identify stocks that are ideal for long-term, disciplined investing. Not every stock is a candidate for monthly accumulation.

Consistent Growth

We look for stocks with stable Revenue and Profit growth over multiple years.

Low Debt

Companies with minimal debt are more resilient during market downturns.

High ROE

A high Return on Equity indicates efficient management and compounding power.

Note: "Excellent" suitability means the stock meets all fundamental criteria for wealth creation through long-term compounding.
